<h1>Lotus Notes: BlackBerry&reg; Enterprise Mobile User</h1>
<h2>The  BlackBerry&reg; Enterprise Mobile User product from Porsche Informatik allows real-time data synchronisation between your Lotus Notes data and your BlackBerry&reg; device.</h2>

<p><b>BlackBerry&reg; Enterprise Mobile User</b> allows you to:

<ul>
<li>Receive, read, edit and send your e-mails in real-time worldwide</li>
<li>View, edit and send attachments such as Word, Excel, Powerpoint or PDF documents on the device</li>
<li>Synchronise your calendar entries with your Lotus Notes calendar</li>
<li>Synchronise your Lotus Notes address book</li>
</ul>  
</p>

<p><b>Requirements:</b>
<ul>
<li>An existing Porsche Informatik e-mail account</li>
<li>The <b>BlackBerry&reg; Enterprise service</b> must be supported by your mobile network operator</li>
<li>A device that supports the BlackBerry&reg; Enterprise service (e.g. BlackBerry&reg; 8800, BlackBerry&reg; Curve 8310, BlackBerry&reg; Pearl 8100)</li>
</ul>    
</p>

<p><b>Running costs:</b><br>

The costs for the BlackBerry&reg; Enterprise Mobile User product payable to Porsche Informatik are 10 Euro/month per user. The costs for use of the BlackBerry&reg; Enterprise service on your mobile network are not included and must be arranged with your mobile network operator. 
</p>
