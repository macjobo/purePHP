<p><b>Announcement: 3rd Infrastructure Workshop 12th -13th  April 2010</b><br />
The next workshop is focussed on desktop management. Together we will develop solution concepts for software distribution, patch management and initial installation topics. Our aim is to assemble an international project team to handle the conception and realisation of a desktop management solution. Andreas Kronberger will lead and coordinate this team.</p>
